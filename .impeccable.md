# Design Context — 綺麗漫画 · KireiManga

## Target audience
Desktop manga readers who care about the experience: anime fans with local CBZ libraries, daily MangaDex readers, Japanese learners reading raw manga, and existing users of the Shiro suite (ShiroAni, Shiranami). Users expect polish on par with Apple/Arc/Linear — not "another Electron app."

## Use cases
- Long-form daily reading sessions (hours at a time) — the UI must get out of the way
- Browsing and discovering new series on MangaDex
- Tracking reading progress across a personal library of 10–2000 series
- Eventually: reading raw Japanese manga with an OCR + translation overlay

## Brand personality / tone
**Calm, editorial, ink-and-paper restraint.** Japanese-inspired without weeb kitsch — no neon, no anime girls, no sakura petals. Think: a thoughtful book-reader's library, a museum wall label, a literary magazine. Generous negative space. Confident typography. Monochrome neutrals with a single restrained accent drawn from traditional Japanese ink pigments (sumi black, bengara red, or aizome indigo — pick one, use sparingly).

## Aesthetic direction
- **Editorial / literary** over "app dashboard." Layouts should feel composed, not gridded.
- **Dark-first** but paper-like — warm off-blacks and ivory, not cold slate and pure white.
- **Typography carries the identity.** A distinctive serif display (something with character — Fraunces, Newsreader, or a Japanese-aware face like Shippori Mincho) paired with a restrained geometric sans for UI (Geist, Söhne-alt, or Manrope). Never Inter.
- **Vertical rhythm matters.** The app is about reading; spacing, leading, and measure should reflect that.
- **One memorable detail**: a subtle Japanese motif — a vertical title rail, a single kanji glyph as a wordmark, a hairline rule inspired by tategaki layout. Pick one; don't stack them.

## Non-goals
- No glassmorphism, no gradient text, no neon glows
- No "card grid of same-size cards with rounded icons" aesthetic
- No dashboard-style metric tiles
- No bouncy animations — decelerate smoothly
